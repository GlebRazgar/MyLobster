What is EasyClaw?

EasyClaw is managed cloud hosting for OpenClaw — the open-source AI assistant that's taking the developer world by storm (80k+ GitHub stars and counting).

We handle the servers, updates, and maintenance. You get a private, always-on AI assistant that you fully control — without touching a terminal.

Think of us as the easy button for self-hosted AI.
