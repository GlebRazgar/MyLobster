Frequently asked questions

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw (also known as Clawd, Moltbot, or Clawdbot) is an open-source AI assistant that can control your computer, automate tasks, and work across multiple platforms.

Is this an official OpenClaw service?

EasyClaw is an independent cloud hosting service for OpenClaw. It is not officially affiliated with the OpenClaw team.

How do I get started?

Choose a plan, complete checkout, and your OpenClaw instance is provisioned for you—no terminal or setup required.

How does billing work?

You pay for your selected plan and can cancel anytime. Ongoing billing follows your plan cycle (monthly or yearly).

How is my data handled?

Each user gets an isolated instance. Data is private, and your environment is separated from other users.

What if I want a refund?

Refund terms depend on launch status and policy; users can contact support for assistance.
