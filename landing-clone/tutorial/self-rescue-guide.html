Self-Rescue Guide: What to do if your EasyClaw instance gets stuck

Because OpenClaw is a powerful agent with deep system-level permissions, it occasionally performs complex operations that might lead to a system loop or a temporary freeze. If your instance stops responding, don’t worry—here is your quick Self-Rescue Guide to get back online in minutes.

1

The Soft Restart

Go to your Dashboard sidebar, find the Server section, and click Restart. This solves most minor hanging issues.

2

The Configuration Reset

If a restart doesn’t work, use the Reset button in the same Server section.

Note: Do not worry—your agent’s stored memory and conversation history are safe and will not be deleted during a reset.

3

The Nuclear Option — Delete & Recreate

If a reset still doesn’t fix it, you can delete the instance entirely and create a fresh one. Go to the Server section and click the Delete button to permanently remove the current instance.

After deleting, return to your Dashboard and create a new instance under the same plan. You’ll get a clean environment and can set up your agent from scratch.

Note: This will permanently erase all data, including your agent’s memory, conversation history, and custom configurations. Only use this as a last resort.

4

Bring in the "Doctor" (Opus 4.6)

Once the server is back up (after a Reset or a fresh Recreate), go to AI Settings in the sidebar and switch your primary model to Opus 4.6.

Opus 4.6 is currently our most powerful model; it acts like a system expert that can diagnose and fix complex technical glitches that other models might struggle with.

5

Restore Your Settings

Send a message to your agent in the chat interface. Ask it to "Restore my Telegram connection and other custom settings." Opus 4.6 will quickly reconfigure your environment to your preferred state.

Follow these steps in order—from a simple Restart, to a Reset with Opus 4.6 recovery, and finally Delete & Recreate as the last resort. This covers virtually every issue you may encounter. As always, if you need further assistance, our team is here to help!
